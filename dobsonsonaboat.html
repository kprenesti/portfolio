DobsonsOnABoat.com | A Wedding Website

DobsonsOnABoat, my very first full-fledged project, was created using Bootstrap 3, SASS, and jQuery.The purpose of the website was to provide a point of reference for the client's wedding guests, many of whom were first-time cruisers. The client wanted a website that would provide all the information their guests would need for their destination wedding as well as tips and information about cruising in general and about the Norwegian Escape in particular. They also wanted their guests to learn about their story, but did not want to write a narrative explaining their story, so they sent me pictures and through ample discussions, I was able to piece them together with a whimsical narrative telling the story of their romance.
